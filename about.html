Who I Am

I'm more than a musician — I'm a sonic architect. Born somewhere between chaos and melody, I weave emotion, texture, and rebellion into every track. My sound doesn't fit neatly into boxes; it breaks them.

From late-night studio sessions to stages soaked in smoke and light, every moment is fuel. I make music for the restless, the dreamers, the ones who feel too much and say too little.
